Tamil Nadu State Traffic Control

Citizens who commit those crimes that violate our Honrobale State - Tamil Nadu's traffic rules are charged under various penal sections and are subjected to pay the appropriate fines that are alloted to them respectively, they are asked to pay the respective fine money either through online or through offline on or before 20 days after commiting the crime, or else the vehicle under action will be seized and heavy fines upto 10000 can be asked.

What can all be done in TNSTC website?

Pay fine online

Fine can be paid whenever wherever and however you want, but on time.

Check previous Crime History

You can verify your previous violations of traffic rules.

Post complaints

If you have any complaints or queries regarding any illegal activities by an officer, you can post here.

Stay up-to-date

New laws can be seen in this website.
